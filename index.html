Meet Sad Night Dynamite, or short, SND. The duo consists of two close friends, Archie and Josh,
who founded SND after leaving school and ultimately managed it purely on a virtual basis when they
were separated at University. The two-piece, whose musical style sounds like a blend of The Specials,
The Avalanches and experimental Hip-Hop elements, self-produce songs that you certainly won’t hear
in your mainstream radio station – instead, they are aiming to create music that portrays disruption,
equality and their belief that there’s a wonderland to get lost in. Just recently, they released
a brand new song alongside a trippy, self-produced video – titled ‘Icy Violence’, SND once again
demonstrate their extraordinary approach to music with a strong statement. Being the first release off their
upcoming debut mixtape, it’s only the beginning of an unpredictable, unique and complex project.
